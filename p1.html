Lee Jeno

Who is Lee Jeno?


Jeno is a South Korean rapper, dancer, singer, and songwriter born in Incheon on April 23, 2000 and later grew up in Ilsandong-gu, Goyang-si, Gyeonggi-do. He is the main rapper, lead dancer, sub-vocalist of NCT DREAM and NCT U.
